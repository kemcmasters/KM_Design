HERMAN MILLER SUMMER PICNIC

PROBLEM:

Design a poster with the style and influence of the Herman Miller Picnic poster competition.

SOLUTION:

By using bold colors, zoomed in imagery, and simple typography, this strawberry poster carries on the Herman Miller style. The strawberry poster also includes an invitation to the picnic. The end of the strawberry is perforated and would be folded over the body of text when sent. The strawberry does break off at fold so guest can bring the schedule with them to the event.
