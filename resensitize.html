reSENSITIZE

PROBLEM:

Find a way to communicate the information and experiences gathered in Iceland to the public back in the states. This not only acts as a blog and gallery space for our images, but it allows our group reSENSITIZE to keep our peers up to date on the upcoming exhibition in January.

SOLUTION:

This reflexive and responsive website was designed to reach people on all types of devices ranging from mobile devices to large desktops. The tabs along the top make the site easy to navigate and icons and jump-links allow the viewer to jump to the desired blog entries.
