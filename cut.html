NEED

PROBLEM:

Use digital photography to explore any subject of interest.

SOLUTION:

With a combination of my pervious photography, new images, and the use of installation, the piece “Need” was created. This project explores Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs, the most basic three being: Physiological, Safety, and Love/Belonging. Each collage includes pictures from Ecuador, Argentina, and the United States to give a comparison of how people around the world are able or struggle to meet these basic needs.

Each collage also has small cut outs that are not easily seen when looking at the images, but when the light is shown through, then the word appears on the wall behind it.
