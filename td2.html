THEATRE
& DANCE: 2014-2015 SEASON

PROBLEM:

After the 2013-2014 season, a similar style was desired for the 2014-2015 season, but we were in search of a new twist.

SOLUTION:

The decision was made to continue with a Herman Miller style, but with a few things mixed in. Instead of the variety of bold colors, each show would have its own spot color, tints and shades of the main color, along with black and white. The clean lines also were broken up with hand drawn illustrations.
